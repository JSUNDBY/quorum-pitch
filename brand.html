Self-Governed. AI-Supported.

LibertyOne is a calm tool for a loud job. Every brand decision — color, type, voice, silence — should reinforce that a neighbor, not a corporation, is on the other end of the screen.

Logo

The mark is a shield with a connected node network — representing governance backed by intelligent infrastructure. Use the wordmark in product contexts; use the mark alone for app icons, favicons, and tight spaces.

Color

Navy anchors authority and trust. Blue does the interactive heavy lifting — buttons, links, active states. Amber is a rare, intentional accent for warnings and warmth — never used twice on the same screen.

Typography

System sans-serif by default (SF Pro on Apple, Segoe UI on Windows). No custom fonts — performance is calm, too. Body text is 19-20px so it reads easily at arm's length, for every reader.

Voice

Warm, plain, literate. Never salesy. Never corporate. Write the way you'd actually speak to a retired teacher who's volunteering to run the community.

Do
"Good morning, Sarah. Your balance is zero. You're all caught up."
Don't

Do
"Sent to 412 residents — switch to Resident to see it."

Do

Don't
"There are currently no outstanding invoices on your account."

Design principles

1. Calm is a feature.

Every pixel earns its place. White space is a design element, not a waste. If it isn't helping someone do something, it shouldn't be on the screen.

2. Write for a neighbor, not a user.

No "utilize," no "assessment cycle," no "please be advised." Say what you mean like a person would say it at the mailbox.

3. The resident is the hero.

The board exists to serve residents. The software exists to serve the board. Every feature decision answers: does this make Sarah's life easier?

4. One action per screen.

When a resident opens a page, they should know the single most important thing they could do there, without reading anything. Then the rest is context.

5. The board gets audited by the state. We get audited by the board.

Every action leaves a trail. Every vote is timestamped. Every dollar is accounted for. Trust is built quietly.
